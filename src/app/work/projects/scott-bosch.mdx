## Overview

A long-term personal engineering project centred around maintaining, servicing, and upgrading a Scott E-Genius 2017 e-mountain bike equipped with a Bosch Performance CX motor. Riding weekly on the Golfie and Glentress trails in Peebles pushed the bike hard, which meant developing a systematic approach to keeping it trail-ready, from motor internals to drivetrain wear.

## Key Features

- **Bosch Performance CX Motor Servicing**: Diagnosed error codes and resolved recurring issues with the Gen1 Bosch Performance CX motor, including annual bearing replacements and general cleaning.
- **Drivetrain Maintenance**: Regularly serviced the full drivetrain including chain, cassette, chainring, and derailleur, managing wear patterns from trail riding.
- **General Servicing & Diagnostics**: Developed a structured diagnostics workflow for identifying mechanical and electrical faults, drawing on online forums, community knowledge, and hands-on trial and error.

## Technologies Used

- **Bosch Performance CX (Gen1)**: Deep familiarity with the motor's internals, failure modes, and maintenance requirements.
- **Drivetrain Components**: Hands-on experience with Shimano/Sram drivetrain systems from tuning, cable tensioning, and wear assessment.
- **Diagnostic Research**: Cross-referencing forum knowledge, technical documentation, and real-world testing to identify and resolve faults.

## Challenges and Learnings

The Gen1 Bosch Performance CX motor, while reliable, requires bearing replacements at least once a year under heavy trail use. Sourcing tools, learning the correct procedure, and carrying out the work without damaging sensitive frame and motor components was the steepest part of the learning curve. Beyond this, managing drivetrain wear on a high-torque e-bike is an ongoing challenge: the added motor assistance accelerates chain and cassette wear significantly compared to an analogue bike. Each fault, a rattly motor, a slipping chain, or unusual motor resistance, became an exercise in systematic diagnosis before reaching for replacement parts.

## Outcome

The bike has been kept in consistent trail-ready condition through self-serviced maintenance, avoiding costly workshop fees and building a thorough understanding of e-bike drivetrain and motor systems.
